Discover 3 Effective Strategies to save money with battery Storage Implementation.

Homeowners are turning to solar power and battery storage to meet their power needs. Batteries in solar systems can save homeowners money in three ways.

  • Electricity prices fluctuate according to time-of-use (TOU) rates offered by many electricity companies. When you store solar power, you can use it when it is cheaper, optimizing your use. This can also ease the demand on the grid during peak times.
  • The energy you capture from the sun can be stored for later use. Without it, extra energy flows back into the grid, resulting in lower-value credits. Stored energy reduces the need for grid electricity and can reduce electricity prices.
  • An emergency power source during outages is battery storage. Imagine your solar panels charging your batteries throughout the day. The batteries could power blackouts without generators or backup power sources.

These benefits will be available to you if you include battery storage in your solar system.
